ABOUT US

The primary objective of a Center for Entrepreneurship is to foster innovation, encourage entrepreneurial thinking and facilitate the creation and growth of new businesses. These centers typically offer a range of programs, services, and initiatives aimed at nurturing entrepreneurship skills

Mentorship and Guidance: Entrepreneurs can benefit from the guidance and expertise of experienced mentors who provide advice, support, and industry-specific knowledge. Entrepreneurship centers connect entrepreneurs with mentors who can offer valuable insights and help them navigate challenges.
